San Francisco is a city in the west coast of California that was founded on June 29, 1776. San Francisco is a diverse city with people from many different backgrounds. San Francisco is known for being a city of many things. It is know for being a technology hub with many companies being headquarted in the prestigious Silicon Valley like Google, Apple, Meta and more. San Francisco is known for its prestigious Universities like the University of California Berkeley and Stanford University, some of my top dream universities. San Francisco has many great sports teams like the Golden State Warriors of the NBA, the San Francisco 49ers of the NFL, the San Francisco Giants of the MLB, the San Jose Sharks of the NHL and more.
